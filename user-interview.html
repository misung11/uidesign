User Interviews

Interview 1

1. What is currently your biggest interest or task?
Online shopping with early morning delivery.
2. How do you relate to this interest or task?
I shop for household groceries and daily necessities as a homemaker.
3. How are you currently approaching this interest or task?
I mostly use Coupang, especially Rocket Delivery and Wow Service.
4. How much time do you typically dedicate to this interest or task?
About 20-30 minutes per day.
5. Can you share about your most recent experience with this interest or task?
The day before yesterday.
6. What aspect of this interest or task do you particularly enjoy?
I like the next-day delivery feature of early morning delivery for gifting.
7. What are the biggest challenges related to this interest or task?
The Coupang gifting feature requires an extra click, and the contact search function is not very efficient. Also, it doesn't sync well with my saved delivery addresses.
8. Why do you continue to engage with this interest or task? What makes it important to you?
Using the gifting feature earns me reward points.
9. What kind of surrounding activities have you established to aid with this interest or task?
I simply manually select the recipient and input the delivery address since the gifting feature doesn't work seamlessly.
10. What is the most difficult part of this interest or task?
Having to click twice for the Coupang gifting feature, inefficient contact search, and lack of synchronization with saved addresses.
11. What are you currently doing to make this interest or task easier?
For gifting, I select manually and input the delivery address separately.
12. Have you tried any other products or tools (applications, etc.) related to this interest or task?
I've used the gifting feature on Naver Shopping.
13. What do you like or dislike about these other products or tools?
Naver has a reasonable font size, and the gifting and purchasing buttons are prominent. However, adding items to the cart is prioritized, making it difficult to see what I've added.

Interview 2

1. What is currently your biggest interest or task?
Idol
2. How do you relate to this interest or task?
fan
3. How are you currently approaching this interest or task?
Watching idol videos on Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, and participating in voting on apps.
4. How much time do you typically dedicate to this interest or task?
About 4-5 hours per day.
5. Can you share about your most recent experience with this interest or task?
Yesterday at 1 a.m.
6. What aspect of this interest or task do you particularly enjoy?
Being able to quickly see and comment on idol photos or videos online, even if just through a mobile device, and being able to support them.
7. What are the biggest challenges related to this interest or task?
Eye strain and discomfort in the neck and shoulders from watching videos on the phone.
8. Why do you continue to engage with this interest or task? What makes it important to you?
It's healing for me to watch idol videos while having meals after work.
9. What kind of surrounding activities have you established to aid with this interest or task?
Watching videos on a smart TV to avoid eye strain and using a stand to hold the phone.
10. What is the most difficult part of this interest or task?
Using the remote control to navigate YouTube on the smart TV, especially for commenting and searching.
11. What are you currently doing to make this interest or task easier?
I comment using my phone.
12. Have you tried any other products or tools (applications, etc.) related to this interest or task?
I mainly use voice search on the smart TV remote control.
13. What do you like or dislike about these other products or tools?
Using a phone for commenting is more convenient, but voice search on the smart TV is faster and more accurate, though it lacks features like commenting or sharing videos.
